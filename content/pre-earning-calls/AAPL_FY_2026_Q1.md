# Pre-Earnings Call Financial Statement

Apple, an innovative provider of PC, OS X operating system, professional applications and digital media, reported its record fiscal Q1'26 financial results:

* Revenue was $143.8B (increased 40.4% QoQ, increased 16% YoY)
* Net Revenues by category:
   1. iPhone: $85.3B, increased 23% YoY
   2. Mac: $8.4B, decreased 6.7% YoY
   3. iPad: $8.6B, increased 6.3% YoY
   4. Wearables, Home and Accessories: $11.5B, decreased 2.2% YoY
   5. Services: $30.0B, increased 14% YoY
* Gross margin was 48.2%, compared to 46.9% in the year-ago quarter
* Net income $42.1B, compared to net income of $36.3B in the year-ago quarter and net income of $27.5B in the previous quarter
* DOI keeps approximately 5 days (estimated based on strong inventory management)
